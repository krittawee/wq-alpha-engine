<objective>
Build the Decay Monitor: decay_monitor.py with detect_decay() logic and run_decay() orchestrator, plus decay.py CLI entrypoint. This delivers OPT-02 end-to-end.

Purpose: Users run `/decay` manually to re-check their PASS and submitted alphas, see which have degraded, and get a Decay note in the vault. The monitor appends time-series rows to checks_history, then compares the latest two rows to flag metric degradation beyond a configurable threshold.

Output: decay_monitor.py (service module), decay.py (CLI entrypoint).
